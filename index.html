How to Use Easy Splittr

1

Enter Transaction Total

Type the total amount of your transaction in the input field. This is the amount you want to split.

2

Choose Split Method

Use Quick Split buttons (50/50, 60/40, 70/30) for common splits, or manually add splits with custom percentages.

3

Assign Categories

Select a category for each split from the dropdown. These help you remember what each split is for.

4

Copy Amounts to YNAB

Click the Copy button next to each amount to copy it to your clipboard, then paste into YNAB.

Features

Save Presets

Save your frequently used split configurations. Click "Save" to name and store a preset.

Copy Category

Click the copy icon next to any category dropdown to copy the category name for pasting into YNAB.

Swap Splits

Quickly reverse the order of your splits with the swap button.

Custom Categories

Add your own categories that match your YNAB budget categories.

Cloud Sync

Sign in to sync your presets and categories across all your devices.

Dark/Light Mode

Automatically follows your system theme, or toggle manually with the button in the top right.

Split by People

Quickly divide equally between any number of people (2-10).

Amount Mode

Switch between percentages and fixed dollar amounts with the mode toggle.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Press ? for help, 1/2/3 for quick splits, Ctrl+S to save preset.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Overview

Easy Splittr is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email address and encrypted password.
  • User Preferences: Your saved presets and custom categories are stored to sync across devices.
  • Local Storage: Theme preferences and data are stored locally in your browser.

Data We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect or store your transaction amounts
  • We do not access your YNAB account
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

Data Storage

If you create an account, your data is securely stored using Supabase with encryption at rest. Without an account, all data stays in your browser's local storage.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Local storage can be cleared through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Used for authentication and data storage
  • Ko-fi: Optional donation widget

Contact

For privacy concerns or data deletion requests, please contact us through the Ko-fi support button.
